# 🎭 Hide and Think
**The ultimate digital "slips of paper" party game.** *Hide your identity, think fast, and bluff your way to victory!*

Hide and Think is a social deduction game designed for both local and remote play. Inspired by the classic "paper in a hat" game, it turns your smartphone into a tool for deception and investigation.

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Join the Room:** Host a local "Pass-and-Play" session or create an online lobby with a unique room code.
2. **The Prompt:** Choose between Random questions, Community Voting, or Custom Inputs where players write the questions themselves.
3. **The Secret Ink:** Everyone submits their answer secretly.
4. **The Debate:** An answer is revealed anonymously. You have 3 minutes to debate:
   - 🕵️ **The Author:** Must bluff and misdirect to avoid being caught.
   - 🔎 **The Detectives:** Must analyze styles and arguments to find the real author.
5. **The Verdict:** Everyone votes. The truth is revealed, and points are awarded!

---

## 🏆 Scoring & Rules

- **🔍 The Detective (+1 pt):** Earn 1 point for every correct vote identifying the true author.
- **🎭 The Master Bluffer (+3 pts):** If you are the author and nobody (or not enough people) votes for you, you earn 3 points for a successful deception.
- **🤝 The Duplicate Rule:** If multiple players submit the exact same answer, they are grouped. Points are awarded once to keep the game fair and prevent "score-padding" through common answers.
